Services

The following is a brief overview on the conduct of our work here at TAC:

  • Income Tax Returns:

    • Individual:

      • Initial client contact and interview
      • Collect all relevant details and information
      • Preparation of working papers
      • Preparation of individual income tax returns
      • preparation of tax schedules - interest received, dividend received, work and other deductions including motor vehicle expenses, work related expenses, home office depreciation, rental property schedules(s), capital gains tax, medical expenses, tax offset such as private health insurance, zone and overseas forces, 20% medical expenses and spouses details and financial information.
      • Review Individual Tax Return with client
      • Lodge Individual Tax Return
      • Review and check assessment and follow up and resolve any differences

    • Partnership:

      • Initial client contact and interview
      • Collect all relevant details and information
      • Preparation of working papers
      • Preparation of general ledger and Financials from work papers and data files
      • Preparation of partnership tax returns
      • Preparation of tax schedules - business income and expenses, partnership and trusts, rent, interest and dividends, deductions, other - foreign source income, business work sheet, and distribution schedule
      • Review Partnership Tax Return with client
      • Lodge Partnership Tax Return
      • Review and check assessment and follow up and resolve any differences

    • Companies:

      • Initial client contact and interview
      • Collect all relevant details and information
      • Preparation of working papers
      • Preparation of company income tax returns
      • Preparation of tax schedules - relevant interest and dividends schedules, capital allowance schedule
      • Reconciliation of taxable income or loss, financial and other information - loses schedule, franking account
      • Personal services income, and tax calculation.
      • Preparation of Research and development schedule
      • Review Company Tax Return with client
      • Lodge Company Tax Return
      • Review and check assessment and follow up and resolve any differences

    • Superannuation Funds:

      • Initial client contact and interview
      • Collect all relevant details and information
      • Preparation of working papers
      • Preparation of Superannuation income tax return
      • Preparation of capital gains schedule and capital allowance schedule (depreciation) schedule
      • Preparation of interposed entity election schedule
      • Preparation family trust election schedule
      • Preparation of losses schedule and
      • Preparation of superannuation surcharge schedule
      • Lodge Superfund Tax Return
      • Review and check assessment and follow up and resolve any differences

  • Goods and Services Tax

      • Obtain source documents
      • Filter and file source documents in alpha and data order
      • Process data in accounting software
      • Print and check GST transaction report
      • Prepare Business Activity Statement form
      • Prepare and lodge on line electronic BAS
      • Inform client of BAS results, refund or payment and report GST collected and paid, PAYG Withholding and PAYG Instalments, fuel credits Fringe Benefits etc

  • Audits

      • Initial communication with ATO
      • Discuss situation with ATO and ascertain the ATO request and requirements
      • Analyse, evaluate, sort and prepare all documents for taxation audit or report
      • Inform client and communicate the progress of the audit with client

  • Fringe Benefits Tax

      • Initial client contact and interview
      • Collect all relevant details and information, data file and other sources of information
      • Preparation of Fringe Benefits spreadsheet and calculation worksheet
      • Preparation of Fringe Benefits Tax Return
      • Review Fringe Benefit Tax Return
      • Lodge Income Tax Return
      • Review and check assessment and follow up and resolve any differences

  • Compliance issues with lodgement programs

      • Request extension of time for lodgements
      • Arrange payment installment for client
      • Discuss client accounts in respect to lodgements, payments and transactions on Integrated Client Accounts and Income Tax Accounts
      • Preparation and Lodgement of Business Activity Statements, GST, PAYG withholding, PAYG Instalments, Fuel Tax Credits

  • Notices of Assessments

      • Record assessment notices against client income tax returns
      • Check assessments for any differences
      • Reconcile, rectify and make any necessary adjustments to Income Tax Return to reflect the assessment
      • Convey discrepancies and differences to client ensuring that the client fully understands why the differences occurred

  • Objections

      • Prepare all documentation and information relevant to the objection
      • Identify the matter and clearly communicate to ATO the reasons for the objection with supporting explanation and documents

  • All Taxations Office Correspondence

      • Communication with ATO via email, phone, written format, use of portal, in relation to all matters such as Income Tax Accounts, Integrated Client accounts, additional information, missing information etc

  • Research and development

      • Apply with Ausindustry for Research and Development registration
      • Complete Application form
      • Prepare all necessary expense analysis and dissections as per Ausindustry requirements
      • Prepare proper program and adequate system for recording and administering Research and Development costs for all R & D related jobs
      • Prepare Research and Development claim
